What is Piles?

Piles, also known as hemorrhoids, are swollen veins in the rectal area that can cause discomfort, itching, and sometimes bleeding. Depending on their location, piles can be internal or external. Laser treatment offers a minimally invasive and effective solution to this condition.

Symptoms

Itchy rectum
Painful bowel movements
Blood in stool
Swelling near anus

Treatment Options

When home remedies do not work, consulting a specialist becomes necessary. Several treatments are available for piles, with laser surgery being the most effective.

Home remedies
Over-the-counter meds
Non-surgical procedures
Laser surgery

Prevention

High fiber diet
Regular exercise
Staying hydrated
Avoid straining
